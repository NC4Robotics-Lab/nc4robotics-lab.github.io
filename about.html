About

    NC4Robotics Lab is part of KU Center for Autonomous Robotic Systems (KUCARS) focused on advancing autonomous and robotic technologies through four main themes and nine integrated projects over three years. The themes include Autonomous Cars, Aerial Robotics, Marine Robotics, and Industrial Robotics.
